Design engineering,
considered.

Mechanical, electronic, and firmware engineering consultancy. We bring careful thinking to complex problems.

Approach
Clarity first

We start by understanding the problem completely before reaching for solutions. Good engineering begins with good questions.

Considered detail

Every decision — from component selection to system architecture — is made deliberately. We don't 'move fast and break things'; we move carefully and build things that last.

Whole-system thinking

Mechanical, electronic, and software disciplines inform each other. We work across boundaries because real problems don't respect them.

Services
Mechanical Engineering

Product design, mechanism development, and structural analysis — from concept sketches through to production-ready outputs. We work in CAD, FEA, and at the bench, and we know when each is the right tool.

Electronics & Embedded Systems

Circuit design, PCB layout, and sensor integration for connected products and instrumentation. From proof-of-concept breadboards to production-qualified assemblies.

Firmware Development

Firmware for microcontrollers and connected hardware, plus the supporting software; control interfaces, configuration tools, and dashboards. From the silicon, to the screen.

Business Analysis & Process Design

Mapping workflows, identifying bottlenecks, and designing systems that let organisations work the way they actually need to. We translate between engineering teams and the rest of the business.

Production & Automation Systems

Manufacturing process design, test rig development, and automation that bridges the gap between prototype and production. Getting something working once is easy; getting it working a thousand times takes engineering.
